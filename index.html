DWF exists to help communities document their origins, traditions, language, music, and ways of life with dignity, beauty, and ownership.

Who We Are

DIVINA WORLD Foundation was created to preserve cultural heritage through inter-generational storytelling, world music, educational programming, and immersive digital experiences.

By partnering with artists, musicians, elders, youth, technologists, and community leaders, DWF gives communities a way to tell their own story in their own voice and language.

This mission brings together culture, representation, entrepreneurship, STEAM education, wellness, sports, food, arts, and the ethical use of VR, tech, and AI.

Our belief
“By remembering our ancestors, we honor ourselves. By celebrating our roots, we learn who we are.”

What the foundation stands on

These are the core values shaping every initiative, partnership, and community experience.

Culture & Representation

Protecting identity through story, language, art, food, music, and the wisdom of elders and community memory.

Youth & Education

Using STEAM, mentorship, music, and edutainment to help younger generations connect with who they are.

Ethical Innovation

Using VR, AI, and immersive technology in a way that honors communities instead of replacing them.
